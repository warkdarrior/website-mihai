August  18,  2003

I'm back from the 12th USENIX Security Sumposium (Security'03) in Washington DC, where I presented my work with Professor Somesh Jha on malicious code detection. Take a look at my paper and the accompanying presentation. Feel free to contact me if you have any comments or if you are interested in working with us on this topic.

A new article by Steven Robinson from Security Focus is now linked on my IT Law page. It is the 3rd part of the series on U.S. information security law.

My paper on virus detection (titled "Static Analysis of Executables to Detect Malicious Patterns") was accepted at the USENIX Security 2003 symposium. Look for my presentation in Washington D.C., August 4-8, 2003!
