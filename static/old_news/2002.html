Welcome to Mihai's News for 2002!

News
July  23,  2002 I started of transitioning pages from dynamic Javascript ones to statically-generated ones. Hopefully this should make the website compatible with more browsers, as well as make it more available to search engine indexing.
May  18,  2002 The semester is finally over! Yee-haw. In the meantime, I added the start of a Funny Stuff page. Most important, I added a page with several proofs about girls being evil, and girls being good. Check it out!
May  17,  2002 I cleaned up the Calendar page a little bit. Now it also has a list of the latest virus threats, courtesy of Sophos (http://www.sophos.com).
March  3,  2002 A new paper summary is available on the summaries page. The paper is titled Protection and the Control of Information Sharing in Multics, and I read it for the Security Reading group (see UWisc CS Security and Cryptography Research page).
February  17,  2002 I put online the Romanian translation of the poem that ends the play "Twelfth Night" by William Shakespeare. You can read it here . I am not the translator, and unfortunately I don't know who translated it from English.
February  16,  2002 Some of the photos from the Washinton DC trip in January are now online, thanks to Vinod (http://www.cs.wisc.edu/~vg) and Gogul (http://www.cs.wisc.edu/~bgogul). You can view them on the DC trip page.
February  15,  2002 I finally created a Travel page. I added information about the 1999 trip to Europe and 2002 trip to Washington DC. Both pages are still works in progress, but now there is some hope.
January  26,  2002 The presentation I gave to ONR during the MURI meeting in Washington DC, for the WiSA project, is now online. Check it out on the Presentations / Talks page.
