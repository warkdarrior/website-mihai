News
October  4,  1999 I have created a new Contact Info page, which, surprisingly, contains various ways of contacting me online and offline. You can find the Contact Info page at http://www.cs.wisc.edu/~mihai/contact.html .
Also, it is worth noting that I have created and updated a web-based calendar, which can be retrieved from http://www.calendars.net/cals/Mihai. Don't worry, it is also linked from the Contact Info page.
August  29,  1999 Starting a new life in a new place takes time and lots of work. After my trip to Europe (from which notes will be soon online on my Play page), I moved to Madison, Wisconsin, to pursue a Ph.D. degree in Computer Sciences at University of Wisconsin, Madison (duh!). Their CS department is one of the best in the United States. My main research interests are in the programming languages / compiler area. Find out more on my Work page.
April  5,  1999 I have decided to continue working on the site. It took me a while! In the mean time I have applied to graduate schools (you can read the results and my comments on this page, successfully completed by Bachelor's in Computer Science at University of California, Santa Barbara, and planned to rule the world (I cannot give any details, just watch CNN!).
