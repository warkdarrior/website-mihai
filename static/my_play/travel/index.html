Mihai's Travel

Everybody should have to travel outside their country at least once in their life. That would definitely eliminate hate and fear of foreigners. Below I list the various places I've visited, and for which I have photographs to show. The list is not comprehensive, hopefully it will grow!

Most photographs are in JPEG format, to save some space. If you want a certain image in PNG format and better resolution (600 dpi), drop me an email, I might have it stored somewhere.

If someone want to give me a Canon EOS D30 and some travel money, I would be more than happy to travel and do some photojournalism for them!

On with the show...

Harpers Ferry, WV - July 10-13, 2002

A research-related trip to Harpers Ferry, West Virginia uncovers a beautiful and quiet corner of US countryside.

Devil's Lake, WI - July 12, 2002

A Sunday trip to the Devil's Lake State Park in Wisconsin turned out to be a very enjoyable way of spending half of my weekend. The park is spectacular, if only a little overrun by visitors.

Check out the whole story here!

Washington DC - January 12 - 16, 2002

I went to DC for a small conference on software safety and security. Of course, I saw it as an opportunity to visit DC and the surrounding area. More here.

Europe - June to August 1999

After I graduated from UC Santa Barbara (http://www.ucsb.edu), I took a month-and-a-half long trip through Europe, with my friend Geoff (http://www.geoffdavis.com).

On the following page, a whole bunch of photos are displayed, as well as some(what) pithy comments! Needless to say, I highly recommend such a trip - only lack of money prevents me from repeating this trip every summer. Some day...
