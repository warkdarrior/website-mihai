Mihai's Project on the
Interaction between Law and Information Technology

Information technology continuously changes in response to market demands and technological advances. Accordingly, the legal framework attempts to adapt to these changes, either by subsuming the new advances within existing practices or by adding laws to cope specifically with these changes.

This page is meant to collect various references to the intricate interaction between information technology and the legal system.
