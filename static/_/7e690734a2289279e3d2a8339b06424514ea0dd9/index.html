About This Event

Goals

The Security & Architecture Day is a workshop sponsored by the Tri-State region computer security research community for bringing area researchers together, fostering multi-institutional collaborations, and discussing and exchanging our ideas and experiences with security and cryptography research.

This event is jointly organized by the Security Research Group at the IBM T.J. Watson Research Center in Hawthorne and the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science departments at Princeton University. The Computer Science department at Rutgers University is also a participant.
