Mihai's Work

I continuously seek new opportunities to improve my knowledge of computing and my experience in the field. Read my resume to find out more about me.
Throughout the years I have worked on various projects, some ongoing, some completed. There is also some source code you can download there. My papers and presentations contain summaries of my research.
I also keep track of the things I learned and I taught in my academic career. The courses page provides context for some of my projects and ideas.

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Computer Sciences department at University of Wisconsin, Madison.

I got my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from University of California, Santa Barbara. Going to UCSB for school opened the path for many collaborations with the faculty and my friends. I also held various officer positions in the campus student organizations:

  • chair of the student Association for Computing Machinery at UCSB (UCSB chapter and national headquarters).
  • vice-president and temporary president of Tau Beta Pi, the National Engineering Honor Society (UCSB chapter and national headquarters).
  • student representative in the Engineering Student Council, an umbrella organization that coordinates the various Engineering student groups at UCSB.

I attended my first year of college at Universitatea Politehnică Bucureşti.

My highschool years were spent at Liceul de Informatică Bucureşti (now known as Colegiul Naţional de Informatică "Tudor Vianu").
